Welcome to Assignment-2 server.

  • Create User

    POST: level2-ass2.vercel.app/users

  • Get All Users

    GET: api/users

  • Get Single User

    GET: level2-ass2.vercel.app/users/:userId (userId starts from 1 and ends to 10)

  • Delete Single User

    DELETE: level2-ass2.vercel.app/users/:userId (userId starts from 1 and ends to 10)

  • Update Single User

    PUT: level2-ass2.vercel.app/users/:userId (userId starts from 1 and ends to 10)

  • Get All Orders of Single User

    GET: level2-ass2.vercel.app/users/:userId/orders (userId starts from 1 and ends to 10)

  • Add Orders

    PUT: level2-ass2.vercel.app/users/:userId/orders (userId starts from 1 and ends to 10)

  • Get Total Price of all Orders of a user.

    GET: level2-ass2.vercel.app/users/:userId/orders/total-price (userId starts from 1 and ends to 10)
